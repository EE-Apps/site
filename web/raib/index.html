    RAIB Fund Security and Non-Political Neutrality Statement

    RAIB guarantees the full safety of all client funds held within the bank. All monetary assets are stored and processed on a dedicated server infrastructure isolated from physical locations, branches, offices, and political jurisdictions. Under no circumstances does the physical situation around any RAIB facility affect the actual safety of client balances.

    RAIB operates as an independent and politically neutral financial institution. Political movements, elections, regime changes, internal unrest, or diplomatic conflicts have no influence on account ownership, access to funds, or transaction validity.

    Confiscation of funds is possible exclusively in cases of proven fraud. This includes financial scams, abuse of banking mechanisms, intentional currency manipulation, asset duplication, or any actions aimed at destabilizing the RAIB financial system. No other grounds for confiscation exist.

    Temporary freezing or compulsory withdrawal of funds may be applied only as punitive measures against hostile actors and aggressors. Such measures may be triggered solely in the event of the approach of hostile armed or sabotage formations within a radius of one kilometer from Snezhny, the location of the main RAIB headquarters, or in the event of direct shelling, bombardment, or any form of armed attack against Snezhny, or in the case of seizure, nationalization, or forceful takeover of any RAIB branch in any other city or country. These measures do not endanger client assets and do not represent a loss of funds. They are designed to deny aggressors access to financial resources and to protect the integrity of the banking system. Ownership of funds remains unchanged unless fraud is conclusively established.
